Student Bio

Dylan Thomas

Early Years

Dylan Thomas was born in the year 1990. From a young age, he had an interest in electronics. His father would find him playing video games deep into the night, much to young Dylan's chagrin. Throughout high school, it would be common for Dylan to skip a whole night's sleep on Saturday's to play through the latest story, on the newest console. Unfortunately, initial college aspirations fell to the wayside due to an unfocused plan.

Adulthood

After dropping out of college, Dylan's continued interest in electronics followed him. While assembling lifts at his wife's family's business, Dylan loved putting together custom computers, and his interest in the inner workings of programs themselves has lead him to the DU code bootcamp.
